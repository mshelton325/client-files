Your Reference Number: [Job Reference]

  • We will investigate your concern within 24 hours
  • A member of our customer service team will contact you by [Date/Time]
  • If needed, we will schedule a site visit to assess the situation

We value your business and are committed to resolving this matter to your complete satisfaction.

[Your Name]
[Title]
[Company Name]
[Contact Information]

Usage: Send immediately upon receiving any customer complaint or concern (within 2 hours maximum).

TEMPLATE CUSTOMIZATION GUIDELINES

Personalization Fields

  • [Customer Name] - Always use customer's preferred name
  • [Job Reference] - Unique identifier for tracking
  • [Your Name] - Staff member handling the case
  • [Company Name] - Your business name
  • [Contact Information] - Phone, email, address
  • [Date/Time] - Specific dates and times

Tone Variations

  • Formal: Use for business/commercial clients
  • Friendly: Use for residential customers
  • Apologetic: When company error is identified
  • Empathetic: For frustrated customers
  • Professional: Standard business tone

Urgency Adaptations

  • Low: Standard timeline and language
  • Medium: Expedited response times
  • High: Same-day response required
  • Critical: Immediate phone follow-up

Industry Considerations

  • Residential: More personal, family-focused
  • Commercial: Business impact emphasis
  • Luxury: Premium service language
  • Warranty: Include technical details

TEMPLATE USAGE INSTRUCTIONS

When to Use Each Template

0-2hrs: Immediate Acknowledgment
24-48hrs: Investigation Update
As needed: Appointment Scheduling
Post-assess: Resolution Proposal
Post-work: Completion Notification
1-2 weeks: Follow-up Satisfaction
If needed: Escalation/Delay
Final: Case Closure

Process Requirements

  • Documentation: Log all communications in customer file
  • Approval: Manager review for escalations and compensation
  • Follow-up: Schedule appropriate next actions
  • Tracking: Update case status in system
  • Files: Attach photos, documents as needed
  • CC Recipients: Include supervisors when appropriate
