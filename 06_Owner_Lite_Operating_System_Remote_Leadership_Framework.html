Escalation Rule Matrix

URGENT - Immediate Escalation

  • • Safety incidents or injuries
  • • Legal threats or issues
  • • Major customer complaints (>$10K impact)
  • • System failures affecting operations
  • • Employee misconduct (serious)
  • • Cash flow emergencies
Response Time: Immediate (call/text)

STANDARD - Same Day

  • • Performance issues requiring intervention
  • • Customer complaints ($1K-$10K impact)
  • • Scheduling conflicts
  • • Vendor/supplier issues
  • • Team conflict resolution
  • • Process clarifications
Response Time: Within 4 hours (email/message)

NON-URGENT - Monthly Log

  • • Process improvement suggestions
  • • Training requests
  • • Minor customer feedback
  • • Equipment maintenance needs
  • • Team development opportunities
  • • Administrative updates
Response Time: Monthly review meeting
